Introduction to OL/UL

This is actually very simple to understand. The OL/UL elements make a sort of list on the site. Ol stands for Ordered line. Which will bring the numbers down from the numer 1 and onwards.
The other one is Unordered line. This one allows you to to list stuff without numbers, and simply dots. Here are the following examples.

    Ordered Line

  1. Sample
  2. Sample2
  3. Sample 3
  4. You're gay

    Unordered Line

  • Sample
  • Sample 2
  • Sample 3
  • Sample 4
  • You're Gay
